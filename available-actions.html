System Maintenance: Available Actions

The blockchain itself does not have the capability of AUTOMATICALLY triggering a certain event once the condition is met. This panel can be used to listen to and detect potential events. Once a threshold or condition is met, it has to be triggered MANUALLY by users. You perform the on-chain transaction and pay the computation(transaction) fee which updates the status and triggers potential events or syncs the data accordingly.
