Issue Stake Donation

This is effectively the mechanism to gift a stake to a wallet using the protocol treasury funds. This is how contributors can request a donation from the protocol in form of a time-deposited stake. You improve the protocol, and you can request a donation in form of a locked-stake in the protocol. The request must be approved through voting.
